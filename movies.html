My top 4 favourite movies of all time

  • Chappie:


    • Genre: Action, Crime, Drama
    Release date: 6 March 2015 (USA)
    Description: In the near future, crime is patrolled by a mechanized police force. When one police droid, Chappie, is stolen and given new programming, he becomes the first robot with the ability to think and feel for himself

  • Spider-Man:


    • Genre: Action, Adventure
    Release date: 3 May 2002 (USA)
    Description: When bitten by a genetically modified spider, a nerdy, shy, and awkward high school student gains spider-like abilities that he eventually must use to fight evil as a superhero after tragedy befalls his family

  • Step-Up Revolution:


    • Genre: Drama, Music, Romance
    Release date: 3 May 2002 (USA)
    Description: Emily arrives in Miami with aspirations to become a professional dancer. She sparks with Sean, the leader of a dance crew whose neighborhood is threatened by Emily's father's development plans

  • Back to the Future:


    • Adventure, Comedy, Sci-Fi
    Release date: 3 July 1985 (USA)
    Description: Marty McFly, a 17-year-old high school student, is accidentally sent thirty years into the past in a time-traveling DeLorean invented by his close friend, the maverick scientist Doc Brown
